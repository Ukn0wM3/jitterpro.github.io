What is Jitter Pro?

Jitter Pro is a site where you can watch Pros from our community play, learn how to improve your skill, and possibly join us and teach others. We will occasionaly link streams of our Pro players so you can watch and learn. If you believe that you need a one on one lesson to improve, we offer that too. If you want to have a one on one lesson, email us a way to contact you (Discord if possible), the game you want to improve in, and what you want to improve. You can contact us through the contacts tab and ask us about anything on the site.
